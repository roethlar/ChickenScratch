## R-25: AI replacement deletes selected prose before the stream succeeds

### Approach

- Kept replacement operations (`polish`, `expand`, `simplify`) from mutating the editor while the AI stream is in flight.
- Captured the selected text and a structured fingerprint of the selected ProseMirror slice before starting the stream.
- Buffered streamed replacement chunks in memory and committed one replacement transaction only after the stream completed successfully.
- Before committing, verified the stream was not aborted, the project/document or flow context had not changed, the original selected text remained at the same range, and the original slice fingerprint still matched.
- Left `brainstorm` behavior unchanged because it appends after the selected passage rather than replacing it.
